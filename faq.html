FAQs & Guidelines

FAQs

Q: Can I cancel my booking?

A: Yes, cancellations can be made up to 1 hour before the booking time.

Q: Are rooms equipped with power outlets?

A: Yes, all rooms have power outlets for your devices.

Guidelines

  • Bookings are limited to 2 hours per session.
  • Please vacate the room promptly after your session ends.
  • Maintain cleanliness and avoid disturbances.
